Meet LumiSynth

Drop a video or open the camera. STRUCTURE shapes the image, COLOR stacks tone, TRACK finds motion, and every knob responds as you move it.

Start simple: load a clip, pick one STRUCTURE, add one COLOR slot, then turn knobs until the canvas answers.
